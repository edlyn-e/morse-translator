Welcome to the Morse Code Translator.

To start, start typing in the input screen on the bottom half of the translator. Then, once you're ready to encrypt your English text into Morse Code, hit the green button!

If you'd like to decrypt Morse Code, pop it into the input screen - remembering to use a pipe " | " to let the translator know of spaces between words - and, once you're ready, hit the blue button!

Simple, right? Happy translating!
